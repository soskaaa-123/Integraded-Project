French

La Routine Quotidienne

A visual story about Marie's daily routine in French. Léa asks Marie about her daily activities and Marie describes her complete day.

Dialogue Translation

Léa: Bonjour Marie, Comment ça va? (Hello Marie, how are you?)

Marie: Ça va bien Merci, Et toi (I'm fine thanks, and you?)

Léa: Qu'est-ce que tu fais pendant ta routine quotidienne? (What do you do in your daily routine?)

Marie describes waking up at 7h, brushing teeth, breakfast at 8h30, taking medicine, lunch at 15h30 with rice and chicken, doing homework, sports, dinner at 21h with fruits, watching TV, and sleeping at 23h.
